Rules

  1. You have a max of 10 rounds to take as many of your opponents weapons as you can.
  2. To select your weapon, you must click in the item in the kit on the right.
  3. Each tool can only be used three times in a row before it breaks.
  4. Everytime either you or Steve lose a match, a tool is taken and given to the other player.
  5. If you wish to reset the game, click the reset button.
